# Scholarship Programs Reference

This document contains researched information about actual scholarship programs that will be featured on the Bangsamoro Scholarship Portal. Information is sourced from official government websites and news releases.

---

## BARMM Government Scholarships

### 1. AHME - Access to Higher and Modern Education

**Provider:** Ministry of Basic, Higher and Technical Education (MBHTE)

**Also Known As:** Bangsamoro IQ Scholar

**Official Portal:** [ahme-mbhte.bangsamoro.gov.ph](https://ahme-mbhte.bangsamoro.gov.ph)

#### Benefits
| Benefit | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Annual Scholarship Grant | PHP 60,000 per academic year |
| Release Schedule | PHP 30,000 per semester (2 tranches) |
| Duration | 4 years (until graduation) |

#### Eligibility Requirements
- Senior High School (SHS) graduate from BARMM
- Alternative Learning System (ALS) graduate from BARMM
- Filipino citizen
- Resident of Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao
- Combined gross annual family income not exceeding PHP 400,000
- Not a recipient of any other government scholarship

**Special Eligibility (Priority):**
- Qualified MILF/MNLF combatants
- Children of combatants
- Orphans of combatants
- Surviving spouses of combatants
- Internally Displaced Persons (IDPs)
- Indigenous Peoples (IP) community members
- Persons with Disability (PWD)
- Children of solo parents

#### Application Requirements
1. Completed AHME Application Form (online via AHME Portal)
2. PSA Birth Certificate
3. Certificate of Residency (Barangay/Municipal)
4. Senior High School Report Card (Form 138) or ALS Certificate
5. Certificate of Good Moral Character
6. 2x2 ID Photo (white background)
7. Proof of Family Income (ITR or Certificate of Indigency)
8. Certificate of Enrollment (upon acceptance)

#### Program Statistics
- Total slots: 1,400 scholars region-wide per cohort
- Since 2020: Over 7,000 students awarded
- Total funding since inception: Nearly PHP 1.25 billion

**Sources:**
- [MBHTE Official Website](https://mbhte.bangsamoro.gov.ph)
- [AHME Portal](https://ahme-mbhte.bangsamoro.gov.ph/about)
- [BARMM Official News](https://bangsamoro.gov.ph/news/latest-news/mbhte-welcomes-600-new-scholars-under-ahme-sp/)

### 2. BASE - Bangsamoro Assistance for Science Education

**Provider:** Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST)

**Official Website:** [most.bangsamoro.gov.ph](https://most.bangsamoro.gov.ph/scholarships-grants-2/)

#### Benefits
| Benefit | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Monthly Stipend | PHP 8,000 |
| Annual Total | PHP 80,000 (10 monthly installments) |
| Duration | 4-5 years depending on course |

#### Eligibility Requirements
- Natural-born Filipino citizen
- BARMM resident for at least 6 months
- STEM strand SHS graduate with minimum 85% General Average (1st semester, Grade 12)
- Non-STEM strand graduate must be in Top 5% of graduating class
- Combined annual family income not exceeding PHP 250,000 (or PHP 350,000 for families with 4+ children)
- Good moral character
- Physically and mentally fit
- No prior post-secondary education units
- Passer of MOST Bangsamoro Scholarship Grant Examination

#### Priority Courses
Science and Technology courses including:
- Engineering (all branches)
- Computer Science / Information Technology
- Mathematics
- Physics
- Chemistry
- Biology
- Agriculture
- Environmental Science

**Sources:**
- [MOST Scholarships Page](https://most.bangsamoro.gov.ph/scholarships-grants-2/)
- [BASE 2025 Application](https://most.bangsamoro.gov.ph/applications-for-base-and-base-merit-scholarship-programs-for-cy-2025-now-open/)

### 3. BASE-Merit - Bangsamoro Assistance for Science Education (Merit)

**Provider:** Ministry of Science and Technology (MOST)

#### Benefits
| Benefit | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Monthly Stipend | PHP 20,000 |
| Annual Total | PHP 200,000 (10 monthly installments) |
| Duration | 4-5 years depending on course |

#### Eligibility Requirements
- STEM strand SHS graduate with minimum 85% GWA (Grade 12, 1st semester)
- Non-STEM strand graduate with minimum 90% GWA
- Passer of BASE-MERIT Qualifying Examination
- High aptitude in science and mathematics
- Willing to pursue career in Science and Technology fields
- Good moral character
- Physically and mentally fit
- No prior post-secondary education units

#### Priority Courses
Advanced Science and Technology courses:
- Physics
- Chemistry
- Biology
- Mathematics
- Geology
- Computer Science
- All Engineering disciplines
- Medical Sciences

### 4. Bangsamoro Medical Scholarship Program

**Provider:** Ministry of Health (MOH-BARMM)

#### Benefits
| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Tuition | Full tuition coverage |
| Other Expenses | School fees and related expenses covered |
| Duration | Full medical degree (4-6 years) |

#### Eligibility Requirements
- Bangsamoro resident
- Graduate of pre-medical course
- Willing to render return service in BARMM (2 years for every year of scholarship)
- Physically and mentally fit

#### Return Service Obligation
- 2 years of service in BARMM public health facilities or hospitals for each year of scholarship received
- Service in underserved areas prioritized

#### Program Purpose
Address the maldistribution of medical doctors in the region (current ratio: 1 doctor per 4,000 people)

**Partner Institution:** University of Southern Mindanao (USM)

**Sources:**
- [MOH Medical Scholarship News](https://bangsamoro.gov.ph/news/latest-news/ministry-of-health-offers-medical-scholarships-to-bangsamoro-students/)
- [PNA News Article](https://www.pna.gov.ph/articles/1151895)

## National Government Scholarships

### 5. TESDA TVET Programs

**Provider:** Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA)

**Official Website:** [tesda.gov.ph](https://www.tesda.gov.ph)

#### Benefits
| Benefit | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Training Cost | Free |
| Training Support Fund (TSF) | PHP 160 per training day |
| STEP Allowance | PHP 60 per training day |
| Assessment Fee | Free |
| Certification | Free |
| Starter Toolkit | Provided upon completion |

#### Programs Available
1. **TWSP** - Training for Work Scholarship Program
2. **PESFA** - Private Education Student Financial Assistance
3. **STEP** - Special Training for Employment Program

#### Eligibility Requirements
- At least 18 years old
- High School graduate or ALS completer
- Filipino citizen
- Not currently employed in a similar field

#### Duration
- Short-term courses: 3-6 months
- Varies by program

#### Priority Courses
- Computer Systems Servicing
- Electrical Installation
- Automotive Servicing
- Welding
- Construction
- Tourism (Housekeeping, Food & Beverage)
- Agriculture
- Health Care Services

**Sources:**
- [TESDA Scholarships Page](https://www.tesda.gov.ph/about/tesda/1279)
- [TESDA FAQ](https://www.tesda.gov.ph/About/TESDA/25687)

### 6. CHED Tulong Dunong Program (TDP)

**Provider:** Commission on Higher Education (CHED) via UniFAST

**Official Website:** [unifast.gov.ph](https://unifast.gov.ph/tes.html)

#### Benefits
| Benefit | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| Financial Assistance | PHP 7,500 per semester |
| Annual Total | PHP 15,000 |
| Duration | Until graduation (renewable annually) |

#### Eligibility Requirements
- Filipino citizen
- Enrolled in first undergraduate degree program
- Enrolled in CHED-recognized Higher Education Institution (public or private)
- Combined gross annual household income not exceeding PHP 400,000
- Not a beneficiary of TES, CHED Scholarship Programs (CSPs), or other national government-funded StuFAPs

#### Covered Expenses
- Tuition fees (partial)
- School expenses
- Living costs
- Education-related expenses

#### Application
- Online application through CHED UniFAST portal
- First-come, first-served basis
- Limited slots available (1,000+ general slots, plus 4 slots per barangay)

**Sources:**
- [CHED Tulong Dunong Info](https://chedscholarship.com/ched-tulong-dunong-scholarship/)
- [DBM Budget Release](https://www.dbm.gov.ph/index.php/management-2/2763-free-college-education-in-ph-dbm-releases-p3-744-billion-for-tulong-dunong-program)
